What is Touch?

History

  • Evolved from Rugby League (Australia, 1960s)
  • Global community of over 40 countries

How Touch Differs

Touch Rugby
Minimal contact Full contact
6 players on the field 7 - 15 players on the field (depending on format)
Continuous (rolling) substitutions Managed substitutions
6 touches > change of possession Unlimited possesion (Rugby Union)

Benefits

  • Minimal contact
  • Suitable for all ages & genders
  • Maximum player involvement
  • Team sport attributes
